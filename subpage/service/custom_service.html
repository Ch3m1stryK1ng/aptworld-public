    FAQ

    Can you make parts without 3D/2D drawings?

    Yes. We support reverse engineering from samples and provide manufacturable drawings for approval.

    What is the MOQ?

    Small batches supported, starting from 1 piece. Tiered pricing for larger quantities.

    How do you guarantee lead time?

    Milestones across material, casting, machining, finishing, inspection, and shipping. Delay discount policy available.

    Do you support third-party inspection?

    Yes, BV/SGS on-site inspection supported. We can also follow your inspection plan.
